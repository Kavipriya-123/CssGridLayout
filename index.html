What we are doing now

Drought is a severe natural disaster that affects millions of people and animals worldwide. It leads to water shortages, food scarcity, and extreme environmental degradation. To mitigate its impact and protect both human and animal lives, it is essential to take proactive measures and adopt sustainable practices. Implement rainwater harvesting systems to collect and store rainwater for future use. Promote efficient irrigation techniques, such as drip irrigation, to reduce water wastage in agriculture.
